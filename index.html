What’s On

Deckchair Cinema screens films 7 nights a week from 18 April – 18 November 2018. The box office is open nightly from 6:00 pm, with films starting promptly at the advertised time.

 

Deckchair Cinema is operated by the not-for-profit Darwin Film Society. The DFS programs an eclectic mix of films, putting out four 7-week programs per year. Check out Program 4 (24 Sept – 18 Nov) HERE.
